I am a PhD candidate in the Luddy school of Informatics, computing, and Engineering at Indiana University Bloomington and a member of the Uncharted lab. As a graduate research assistant under the supervision of Dr. Lei Jiang, I am involved in designing domain-specific accelerators. My research interests include but are not limited to efficiently accelerating applications such as genome sequencing and neural networks, by utilizing emerging technologis such as ReRAM, superconductore devices and photonic.
